Various projects I have developed, am developing, or am somehow involved in (listed in no particular order).

  • UnWheel
    Driving modification for Unreal Tournament 2004. Winner of several awards, including multiple first places in Epic and NVidia's "Make Something Unreal Contest" and "Driving Mod of the Year" for 2004 and 2005 at ModDB.com. (yes, that was a rather shameless pimp)
  • DynaBar
    Dynamic userbar generator and designer. PHP.
  • Injector
    A web-based comic viewer and manager for Dosage.
  • Image Browser
    A very simple PHP script which presents directories of images as a gallery of thumbnails, with some upload options.
  • Monster Hunt
    Unreal Tournament mod featuring fast-paced co-op stlye gameplay.
  • Out of Eve
    A web application which consumes the Eve Online player API, allowing users to track their in-game data from anywhere at any time.
  • SaveScreen
    Automatically saves images as JPEG images when you press Print Screen, with online upload and publishing features.
  • JSON Explorer
    Windows-based desktop utility for quickly formatting and representing a JSON data structure as an interactive tree of elements, objects and arrays.
